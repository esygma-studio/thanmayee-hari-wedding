How It Works

Our Process

Three focused phases. Seamless collaboration. Invites that exceed every expectation.

01

Understanding Customer Needs

We begin with a conversation — not a form. We want to understand your vision: the style, the mood, the story you want your invite to tell. What colours speak to you? What elements feel like you as a couple? We gather this input with care, so that everything we create is rooted in who you are.

You are always in the loop. We keep communication open from the first message to the final delivery — nothing is ever a surprise.

02

Design

Our designers translate your vision into breathtaking visual designs — each event gets its own personality, its own palette, its own moment. Drafts are shared at each stage so you can see the work evolving and give feedback in real time. We don't move forward until you're happy.

Unlimited revisions are included in every Signature and Grand package — not because they're always needed, but because we want you completely at ease. This is your day. The invite should be perfect.

03

Animation

Once the design is approved, we bring it to life. Our animation team adds motion, transitions, and music to transform your invite from a still design into a cinematic moment — the kind that makes guests stop and watch twice.

The final animated invite is delivered ready to share digitally. It's the very first impression of your big day — and we make sure it's unforgettable.

All prices are subject to confirmation at the time of booking. Packages and add-ons can be freely combined. Reach out to discuss your vision — we would love to be part of your story.
